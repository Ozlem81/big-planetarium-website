Saturn – The Ringed Planet

Saturn is best known for its spectacular ring system made of ice and rock particles.

It is a gas giant and the second-largest planet in the Solar System.

  • Distance from Sun: 1.4 billion km
  • Moons: 140+ known moons
  • Day length: 10.7 hours
