Jupiter – The Giant Planet

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System. It is a gas giant composed mainly of hydrogen and helium.

Jupiter has a strong magnetic field and is famous for its Great Red Spot, a massive storm that has been raging for centuries.

  • Distance from Sun: 778.5 million km
  • Moons: 90+ known moons
  • Day length: 9.9 hours
